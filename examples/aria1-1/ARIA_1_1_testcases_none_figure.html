ARIA role="none" Tests

NOTE ON TEST SUITE Tests were undertaken on these code examples using IE 11, Safari 10 and latest builds of Chrome and Firefox. AT used was JAWS 18, NVDA, and VoiceOver.

The ARIA role="none" attribute is synonymous with the presentation role, due to author confusion surrounding the intended meaning of the word "presentation" or "presentational." Many individuals erroneously consider role="presentation" to be synonymous with aria-hidden="true", and we believe role="none" conveys the actual meaning more unambiguously.
